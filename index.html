Who we are

Hi we are Hilab, a consultant company specializing in hardware in the loop development.

When the automotive industry shifted to be more software defined it was a big change affecting the industry to the core.
Instead of integrating decentralized systems with software included from suppliers, the industry now becomes more software defined with in housed developed software on central ECUs.
This centralized architecture opens up for more flexibility and customer value but also means that all software that is developed needs to be tested and verified to the high standards of today's automotive industry. In other words, the shift has made HIL and SIL test environments more important than ever.
Hilabs founders Eric and David saw the need for software testing and the lack of good competence in the area as an opening to start Hilab.

A consultant company that to its core is about software testing on real hardware, from sensor to actuator.
